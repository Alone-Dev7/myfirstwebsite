Our story

A café with conviction

Brûlée was founded on the belief that excellent coffee is not a luxury — it's what happens when you care about every link in the chain. We work directly with twelve farms across Ethiopia, Colombia, and Guatemala, visiting each harvest and paying a price that reflects the artistry involved.

Our head roaster, Mia Takahari, trained in Tokyo and Melbourne before arriving here in 2017. Every roast profile is developed over weeks, then validated by a panel of six in-house tasters. Nothing reaches the bar without unanimous approval.

The café itself was designed to disappear behind the coffee — no loud music, no Instagram traps, just light, warmth, and the smell of something good.
